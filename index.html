Synthesis Mechanisms

üî¨

Intracellular Synthesis

Metal ions are transported into fungal cells and reduced by intracellular enzymes.

1. Metal ion uptake by cells
2. Intracellular enzyme reduction
3. Nanoparticle accumulation

‚öóÔ∏è

Extracellular Synthesis

Metal ions are reduced by secreted enzymes in the culture medium.

1. Enzyme secretion to medium
2. Metal ion reduction outside cells
3. Easy harvesting from filtrate

Challenges & Limitations

1

Scalability Issues

Difficulty in large-scale production and maintaining consistency in industrial settings.

2

Standardization

Controlling particle size, shape, and uniformity across different batches remains challenging.

3

Time-Consuming Process

Biological synthesis takes 24-72 hours, slower than chemical methods (minutes to hours).

4

Purification Costs

Separation of nanoparticles from fungal biomass and proteins can be expensive.

5

Toxicity Concerns

Long-term toxicity effects and environmental impact need comprehensive research.
